About This Project

Richfield Student Transport Guide - Making student commuting safer and smarter

Our Mission

The Richfield Student Transport Guide is designed to help students navigate transportation options in and around Johannesburg. Our mission is to make commuting safer, easier, and more efficient for students.

Features

  • Interactive map with transportation routes
  • Real-time route directions with visual paths
  • Multi-language support (English, Zulu, Sotho)
  • Secure user authentication system
  • Password reset with email support
  • Account management and deletion
  • Popular route recommendations
  • Professional Richfield university branding
